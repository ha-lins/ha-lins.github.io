Hi, I am currently a second-year master student at the HCP Lab in Sun Yat-sen University, where I am fortunately advised by Prof. Xiaodan Liang to conduct research on natural language processing (NLP). I also work closely with Prof. Zhiting Hu and Pan Zhou.

I have a broad interest in NLP and machine learning problems, especially utilizing deep learning for various pratical NLP tasks. Currently, my major research interest focuses on dialogue system and text generation.

More about me: Google Scholar / Github / Linkedin

Research Interests

  • Low-resource NLP (few-shot learning and cross-domain settings)
  • Dialogue System (especially in medical automatic diagnosis)
  • Controllable Text Generation: data-to-text generation etc.
  • Graph Contrastive Learning.
